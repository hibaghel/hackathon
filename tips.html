Diabetes

Diabetes is a disease in which your blood glucose, or blood sugar, levels are too high.

Symptoms-When symptoms do occur, they include excessive thirst or urination, fatigue, weight loss or blurred vision.

Learn more

Cancer

A disease in which abnormal cells divide uncontrollably and destroy body tissue.

Symptoms-Some patients have abnormal bumps, unexplained fevers, night sweats or unintentional weight loss.

Learn more

Common Cold

A common viral infection of the nose and throat.

Symptoms-Symptoms include a runny nose, sneezing and congestion. High fever or severe symptoms are reasons to see a doctor.

Learn more

Headache

A painful sensation in any part of the head, ranging from sharp to dull, that may occur with other symptoms.

Symptoms-Examples include lack of sleep, an incorrect eyeglass prescription, stress, loud noise exposure or tight head wear.

Learn more

Arthritis

Inflammation of one or more joints, causing pain and stiffness that can worsen with age.

Symptoms-Symptoms include pain, swelling, reduced range of motion and stiffness.

Learn more

AIDS

HIV causes AIDS and interferes with the body's ability to fight infections.

Symptoms- AIDS symptoms include weight loss, fever or night sweats, fatigue and recurrent infections.
